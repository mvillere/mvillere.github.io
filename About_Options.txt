LONG (full professional narrative)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Michael Villere is a Web3 engineer, entrepreneur, and technical lead with deep roots in both
blockchain and enterprise software. He co-founded NFT Culture in 2021 - a media brand and
development studio at the intersection of art, culture, and the blockchain - and has served
as CTO ever since. Across NFT Culture and client work, Michael has deployed 30+ smart contracts
to Ethereum mainnet and EVM-compatible networks, shipped full-stack minting platforms, backend
infrastructure, and open source tooling under the NFT Culture Labs umbrella.

His on-chain work has powered projects for world-renowned artists including Ian Cheng, Leo
Villareal, JR, and Refik Anadol - notably Ian Cheng's adaptive generative portrait 3FACE (2022),
which reads its owner's wallet history to generate a personalized visual identity, acquired by
the permanent collection of the MoMA.

Before moving into Web3, Michael spent 13 years as a senior engineering leader in EdTech,
architecting and managing large-scale SaaS products that collectively served over 15 million
students across approximately 20,000 schools and accounted for ~$50M in annual revenue.
He holds a B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Dallas.
